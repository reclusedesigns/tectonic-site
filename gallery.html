With the challenge of creating a luxury family home on 600 squares of land...

we drew on the charm of the period surrounds incorporating stonework, render, French windows and ironwork on the balcony to capture an “old world” feel on the facade.

These elements were then blended throughout the back kitchen/living area of the home, extending to the outside pool/entertainment areas.

All these elements enhanced the warmth of the American Style Home and drew on its French influence.

  • Things of note:
  • 48 squares building area
  • Northerly aspect design
  • Ironwork on external balcony,pergolas and internal staircase
  • Hydronic heating
  • Parquetry ground floor
  • 4 bedrooms (5th in attic)
  • Formal Study (Further study work area in attic)
